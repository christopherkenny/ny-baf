        AN ACT to amend the state law, in relation to establishing congressional
          districts; and to repeal article 7 of such law relating thereto
 
          The  People of the State of New York, represented in Senate and Assem-
        bly, do enact as follows:
 
     1    Section 1. Article 7 of the state law is REPEALED and a new article  7
     2  is added to read as follows:
     3                                  ARTICLE 7
     4                           CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICTS
 
     5  Section 110. Present congressional districts.
     6          111. New congressional districts.
     7          112. Separability of congressional districts.
     8    § 110. Present congressional districts. The congressional districts of
     9  this  state,  as  existing immediately before the effective date of this
    10  article, shall continue to be the congressional districts of  the  state
    11  until  the  expiration  of  the terms of the representatives in congress
    12  then in office, except for the purpose of an election of representatives
    13  in congress for full terms beginning at such expirations.
    14    §  111.  New  congressional  districts.  Notwithstanding   any   other
    15  provision  of  law  to the contrary, the congressional districts of this
    16  state from and after the effective date of this article,  shall  consist
    17  as follows:
    18    1. Congressional District 1.
    19    Within Suffolk County
    20    All of East Hampton town

    21    §  2. Construction. a. This act shall be liberally construed to effec-
    22  tuate the purposes thereof and to apportion and district this  state  in
    23  compliance  with  constitutional  and  statutory  requirements and shall
    24  supersede any inconsistent provision of law including but not limited to

    26    b. It is intended  that  this  act  and  the  congressional  districts
    27  described  herein  completely encompass all the area of the state. It is
    28  also intended that such districts apportioned on the basis of 2020 popu-
    29  lation, contain the whole number of persons in this state. It is further
    30  intended that the apportionment and districting provided for in this act
    31  result in the creation of districts containing equal population as near-
    32  ly as practicable. It is also intended that no  district  shall  include
    33  any of the area included within the description of any other district.
    34    §  3.  Saving  clause. a. If the districts described in the act do not
